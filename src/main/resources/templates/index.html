Welcome to our Recruitment Platform!

For Employers:

Are you looking to find talented programmers to join your team? Look no further! Our platform provides you with the tools you need to streamline your hiring process and connect with the best candidates. Here's what you can do:

  • Create an Account: Represent your company on our platform by creating an account. Set up your company profile and let candidates know what you're all about.
  • Define Your Ideal Candidate: Tailor your search by specifying the qualities you're looking for in a candidate. From technical skills to soft skills, you can customize your criteria to find the perfect match.
  • Browse Candidate Profiles: Our advanced search feature allows you to explore candidate profiles based on your defined criteria. Get an in-depth view of their skills, experience, and more.
  • Streamlined Communication: Reach out to candidates directly through our platform. Easily manage your interactions and keep track of your conversations, all in one place.
  • Mark Candidates as Hired: Once you've found the right fit, mark the candidate as hired. This lets other companies know that the candidate is no longer available, reducing unnecessary communication.
  • Post Job Offers: Looking for the perfect candidate? Post your job offers on our platform to attract potential candidates directly to your open positions.

For Candidates:

Are you a skilled programmer looking for your next opportunity? You're in the right place! Our platform empowers you to showcase your talents and connect with companies that align with your goals. Here's what you can do:

  • Create Your Profile: Build your virtual CV by setting up your profile. Highlight your skills, experiences, and accomplishments to make a lasting impression.
  • Upload Your Photo and CV: Add a professional touch by uploading your photo to your profile. Let your personality shine through!
  • Search for Companies: Browse through a list of companies actively seeking candidates like you. Filter your search based on various criteria to find the right match.
  • Stay in Control: If you're not currently open to new opportunities, you can easily mark your profile as "inactive." This lets companies know that you're not actively looking, but your information is still available for future reference.
  • Connect with Employers: Receive direct messages from companies interested in your profile. Engage in meaningful conversations and take the next step in your career journey.
  • Explore Job Offers: Browse through job offers posted by employers seeking candidates like you. Find the opportunities that align with your career goals.
